Homelab

A ragtag team of old equipment I've gathered over the years

Before I took my homelab down for my move to Tampa, my homelab consisted of two Raspberry Pis and one Win 10 box running 3 Ubuntu Virtual Machines. I created a K8s cluster of the two Pis and 3 VMs over my home wifi network and toyed with initially hosting this website on that cluster. I quickly learned that Raspberry Pi 2 did not like modern security requirements on WIFI, you can hardcode some of the attributes required but if the NIC goes to sleep or shuts off, it will refuse to negotiate with the router. Hopefully upgrades and a cooler project is coming soon!

Simple, Scalable, and Free

To host both this website and the original version depicted in the first image, three web hosting architectures were meticulously generated and tested to determine the optimal fit.
While each solution proved viable and demonstrated effective scalability, the selected solution was S3 + CloudFront. This decision is attributed to its alignment with AWS' free-tier usage, resulting in reduced costs near 0. Moreover, this solution stands out for its simplicity, ease of maintenance, and global delivery.
